About Our Company

MANAN METAL WORKS

Manan Metal Works, established in 2006 by Mehul Panchal in Gujarat, India, is a leading manufacturer of FIBC, bulk, and jumbo bags. Renowned for high-quality products, we serve both domestic and international markets. Our durable packaging solutions have earned us a trusted name in the industry. With a focus on innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction, we continue to grow and meet evolving global demands, ensuring excellence in every product we deliver.
